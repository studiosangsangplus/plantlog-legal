Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

Plant Log is a plant watering tracker app provided by Studio Sangsang. This Privacy Policy explains how Plant Log handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Store

Plant Log allows users to add plants, set watering cycles, and record watering history.

The following information may be stored on your device:

  • Plant names
  • Plant icons
  • Watering cycle settings
  • Watering records
  • App settings

This information is stored locally on your device. We do not require users to create an account, and we do not operate our own server to collect or store this information.

2. Advertising

Plant Log may display ads using Google AdMob or Google Mobile Ads.

Google and its partners may collect or use certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app interaction data, and approximate information, to provide, maintain, and improve advertising services.

For more information about how Google uses information from apps that use Google services, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. Notifications

Plant Log may send local notifications to remind you when it is time to water your plants. Notification settings are handled by your device operating system. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Plant Log may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads
  • Google Play services

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share plant names, watering records, or app settings with our own server. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may collect or process data according to their own policies.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Plant and watering data is stored locally on your device. You can delete plant data within the app by deleting plants or watering records. You can also remove all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data from your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Plant Log is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
